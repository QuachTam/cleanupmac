SpaceMint – Mac Storage Cleaner

Understand your storage before you clean it.

SpaceMint is a macOS storage utility that helps you find large files, exact duplicates, application leftovers, and space-consuming folders. Nothing is selected automatically, and cleanup always requires your confirmation.

Review your Mac

Analyze accessible folders, discover large files, inspect exact duplicates, and understand where storage is being used.

Optional cloud scans

Connect Dropbox or Google Drive to review cloud file metadata. SpaceMint does not download file contents just to calculate file size.

Cleanup with context

Review names, locations, sizes, and dates before moving selected items to the Mac or cloud provider Trash.

How Google Drive access is used

Google Drive access is optional and starts only after you choose to connect your account. SpaceMint uses the permission to list file metadata needed for storage analysis and duplicate detection, and to move files you explicitly select to Google Drive Trash. SpaceMint does not sell Google user data or use it for advertising.

Designed for transparency

  • Local scans are processed on your Mac.
  • Cloud cleanup requires an explicit review and confirmation.
  • Disconnecting a provider removes its saved credential from the app.
  • You can revoke Google access at any time from your Google Account.
