Need help with scanning, permissions, purchases, or a connected cloud account? Send us a message and include the macOS version and SpaceMint version you are using.

Email SpaceMint Support

Google Drive and Dropbox

If a cloud account cannot connect, confirm that you selected the expected account and allowed the requested access. You can disconnect the provider in SpaceMint and reconnect it. Google access can also be revoked from your Google Account security settings.

File access

The Mac App Store edition scans folders you explicitly authorize. The Direct edition may require Full Disk Access for protected locations. SpaceMint reports unavailable folders when macOS denies access.

Cleanup and recovery

SpaceMint does not select cleanup candidates automatically. Review selected paths before confirming. Local files are moved to the macOS Trash and supported cloud files are moved to the provider's Trash.

Privacy requests

For privacy questions or requests, email smartgroup.tqn.global@gmail.com. Read our Privacy Policy for details about local and cloud data processing.
